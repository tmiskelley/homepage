Video games

Video games are everywhere in popular culture today, and no matter what your preferences there's garunteed to be something you'll like. Below are just a few of my favorite games I've played over the years.


  • Don't Starve

    A rougelike survival game where you must explore and gather resources to survive

  • Subnautica

    An adventure survival/craft game where you must explore and gather resources underwater to survive and progress

  • Factorio

    A base building/resource managment game where you construct a factory to build a rocket silo and escape the planet you are stranded on

  • Civalization 5

    A turn based strategy game where your goal is to become the dominant civilization, either by war, technological advancement, or diplomacy.
